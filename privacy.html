MoneyMiners Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

MoneyMiners is an idle mining game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, email, and password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email.
  • Game Data: Game progress, upgrades, gem collection, prestige level, and gameplay statistics stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • Earnings Data: Cash balance earned from ads, and PayPal email for cashouts.
  • Ad Data: Ad interaction data through Google AdMob.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the game experience and save progress
  • To process cash rewards and PayPal payouts
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To maintain the leaderboard and referral system

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database
  • Google AdMob: Ad serving
  • PayPal: Payment processing for cashouts

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore with encryption. We do not sell your personal information.

Children's Privacy

MoneyMiners is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

  • Delete your account from within the app (My Cabin > Delete Account)
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt out of personalized ads through device settings
