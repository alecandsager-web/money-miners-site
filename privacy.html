MoneyMiners Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

MoneyMiners ("the app") is operated by Alec Andsager. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: If you create an account or sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address. This information is stored securely using Google Firebase Authentication.

Game data: We store your game progress including gems earned, upgrades purchased, prestige level, town progress, streak data, and other gameplay statistics. This data is synced to Google Firebase Firestore so you can access your progress across devices.

Device information: We may collect basic device identifiers for analytics and ad serving purposes.

Ad interaction data: When you watch ads, basic interaction data (such as ad completion) is collected by our ad provider, Google AdMob.

Cashout information: If you request a PayPal cashout, we collect your PayPal email address to process the payment.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Save and sync your game progress across devices via Firebase Firestore
  • Authenticate your account via Firebase Authentication
  • Track your in-game earnings and manage cashout requests
  • Serve ads through Google AdMob, including rewarded and interstitial ads
  • Maintain your login streak and apply streak bonuses
  • Improve the app experience

3. Third-Party Services

MoneyMiners uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For user authentication (Firebase Auth) and cloud data storage (Firestore). See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: For serving rewarded and interstitial advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • PayPal: For processing cashout payments. When you request a cashout, your PayPal email is used to send payment. See PayPal's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Your game data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service. This includes:

  • Game progress (gems, upgrades, depth, prestige level)
  • Town and upgrade ownership data
  • Earnings balance and cashout history
  • Streak and login history
  • Profile preferences

Some data is also cached locally on your device for offline access and performance.

5. Advertising

MoneyMiners displays ads through Google AdMob. You may watch rewarded ads to earn in-game cash rewards, or encounter interstitial ads during gameplay. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

6. Cashouts and Payments

MoneyMiners allows you to earn real money by watching ads. When you reach the minimum cashout threshold, you can request a PayPal payment. We collect your PayPal email address solely to process these payments. Payment processing is subject to PayPal's terms and privacy policy.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen within the app. Deleting your account will permanently remove all game data from Firebase, including gems, upgrades, prestige progress, earnings history, and profile information. Any pending cashout requests should be resolved before deleting your account.

8. Children's Privacy

MoneyMiners is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data transmitted between the app and Firebase is encrypted via HTTPS. Firebase provides enterprise-grade security for stored data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
